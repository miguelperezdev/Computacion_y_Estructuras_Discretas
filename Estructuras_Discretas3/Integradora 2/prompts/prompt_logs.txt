--- Interaction 3 ---
Tool: Claude (claude.ai)
Topic: Tweet-specific NLP preprocessing for sentiment analysis
Prompt submitted:
  "What preprocessing steps are recommended for sentiment analysis on Twitter
   data with RNN/LSTM models? How should URLs, mentions, and hashtags be handled?"
How output was used:
  Informed the design of the tweet cleaning pipeline in src/preprocessing.py.
  We implemented URL removal, @mention removal, and hashtag expansion (#word → word).
  Critical deviation: the AI recommended removing all stopwords, but we chose
  to keep negation words (not, never, no, nor) after verifying empirically that
  removing them degraded model accuracy on the validation set.
